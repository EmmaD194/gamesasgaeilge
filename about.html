ABOUT

These games were all developed and coded by Emma Daly, Computer Science, Irish and Linguistics Student at Trinity College Dublin.

I made these games after deciding to take up Irish in college recently. I hadn't used the language in nearly two and a half years and my notes from school were long gone, but I noticed there wasn't many resources or games online concerning the Irish language. So, I decided to make some games "as gaeilge". Developing these games became a good way to "refresh" the language for me and I enjoyed making them!

I hope you have fun playing these games and if you notice any errors or have any suggestions, please get in touch!
