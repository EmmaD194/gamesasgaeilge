Irish language learning games

These are a few web games to help in the learning and understanding of the Irish Language!

There are some games about Irish verbs and grammar and a few translations of popular board games. More games will be added in the future!

The games work best on a PC/laptop but most of them run on mobile (they just might not look as good!).

GAMES

Grammar Games

Longa Cogaidh

A game of battleships, enter the correct form of the verb to shoot!


Críochnaigh an Briathar

A game where you must determine if the verb matches the verb ending (leathan le leathan, caol le caol!)

Tráth na gCeist

A short quiz testing your knowledge of nouns, adjectives, verbs and prepositions.

Toraíocht Taisce

Enter the correct prepostional pronoun to find the buried treasure!

Word Games

Meaitseáil

Match the verbs to their meaning in this game. Includes a study mode with to help understand the verbs.

Countdown

A game based off the TV show "Countdown" but the letters and words are "as Gaeilge".

An Crochadóir

The classic game of Hangman using keywords from Leaving Cert Irish poetry!

An Crochadóir - Basic Words

The classic game of Hangman using basic Irish words like colours, body parts and animals!

Board Games

Dubopoly

The classic board game but the properties are based on areas in North Dublin. Play with a human or the computer!

Triocha Soicind

Describe the words on the card to your teammates (without saying the word!)



Boggle

Find as many words as you can using the letters in the square. The letters must be beside each other horizontally, vertically or diagonally
